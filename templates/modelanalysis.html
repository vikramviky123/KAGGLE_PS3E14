Model Analysis

1. Ensemble of 4 Models for Training

Training involves the use of four different models for regression:

  • Random Forest Regression
  • Historical Gradient Boosting Machine Regression
  • XGBoost Regression
  • LightGBM Regression

2. Model Training

Model training is performed using k-fold cross-validation with 5 splits. The average of predicted values is computed to evaluate the model's performance.

3. Metrics Chosen

The following metrics are chosen to assess model performance:

  • R-squared (R2)
  • Mean Squared Error (MSE)
  • Mean Absolute Error (MAE)
  • Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)
